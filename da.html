Data Analytics

Activities

  • Conducts interviews to understand the problems, questions, pain points and goals​​
  • Assess data sources and relationships​​​​
  • Plan the analytics product life cycle for your needs​​​​
  • Create personas, reframe questions and metrics and user stories​
  • Review the data, check feasibility of answering questions​
  • Begin ideating for how we can execute​
  • Get feedback on wireframes
  • Focus on creating a working prototype​​
  • This stage will also have cycles of iteration, this time focused on accuracy, interactivity, and data security
  • Verify design fits into the actual work of the business
  • Deliver the product ​
